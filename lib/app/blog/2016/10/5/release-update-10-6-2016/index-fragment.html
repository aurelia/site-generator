Release Update - 10/6/2016

Posted by AureliaEffect on October 6, 2016

Day by day our amazing core team and community contributors continue to make improvements to Aurelia. Below are the notes for what we pushed out in this week's releases.

Release Notes

Bug Fixes

  • Origin.get #41 ( 7b4a0bc ) - Addresses cases where some module loaders export text modules as a string.
