Aurelia Release Notes - Early April 2017

Posted by AureliaEffect on April 12, 2017

As always, we continue to make improvements to Aurelia's core as well as its most popular plugins. This set of releases includes not only bug fixes, but substantial improvements to the Aurelia Webpack Plugin, Aurelia UX, Aurelia CLI and our UI Virtualization Library.

In addition to the improvements we're announcing today, in the next week we'll be making announcements around new Webpack skeletons and our new dialog plugin.
